About Us

Who are we?

We are a fitness club that take your fitness goals to another level and help you achieve them in the most professional ways.

Advance Fitness Gym & Club was founded in 2019 with the begining of the severe economic crisis in Lebanon having a goal to create job opportunities for the youth and an escape for everyone who desires to challenge all the difficulties and resfresh their blood.

Our members are our first priority and their health is our responsibilty, this is why we have engaged the most professional team to help you throughout your journey.
